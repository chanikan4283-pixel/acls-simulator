A - Airway

  • Maintain advanced airway. Confirm with waveform capnography.

B - Breathing

  • Target SpO2: 92–98%. Avoid Hyperoxia.
  • Target ETCO2: 35–45 mmHg. Avoid hyperventilation.

  • Target MAP: >65 mmHg.
  • 12-Lead ECG — evaluate for STEMI / PCI candidate.
  • Consider vasopressors for hypotension.
